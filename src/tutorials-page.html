As a beginner cosplayer, items I've created aren't as outstanding as they could be. Nevertheless, I enjoy experimanting with different materials, methods and ordinary items when recreating an item seen on the character I'm cosplaying.

My main goal is to make a costume as cheap and creative as possible, while retaining its funcionality and durability.

Along the way, cardinal mistakes were made, such as wrong binding agent being used, costume dimensions being off, things falling apart when presenting before the judges etc.

Like everything in life, nothing can be perfect in the first attempt, it is important to learn from mistakes and be proud of the progress that has been made. It is also important to surround yourself with people who share your interests and with whom you can advance together and share memories.
